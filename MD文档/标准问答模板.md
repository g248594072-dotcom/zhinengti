# 大力丸 · 客户问答标准公式模板（v1.0）

> **用途**：客户问什么、怎么答的「公式 + 真实话术范例」。
> **来源**：`聊天记录/` 真实成交对话逐句取证 + `培训_SOP_4.0` + `大力丸销售标准化思维和回复`。
> **配套**：判断对错见 `成交客户聊天判断标准.md`；本文件管「该怎么回」。
> **语言**：客户多为澳洲英文，范例给中文公式 + 英文话术参考，实际按客户语言回。

## 〇、万能应答总公式（所有问题通用）

> 任何客户发言，先套这 4 步，再选下面对应分类的话术填充。

```
1. 接（承接）：先回应客户上一句，让他感到被听见
2. 共情/认可：认可他的顾虑或情况（尤其负面情绪）
3. 给料（对症内容）：用对应分类的「证据/机制/方案」正面回答
4. 推进（留问号）：抛一个问题或下一步，把球踢回给客户
```

**铁律（违反即扣分）**
- 第一性 = 先回客户**当下这句**，不要自顾自发下一段话术
- **每段尽量以问号结尾**，不要发完就停（人设/案例发完必须带引导）
- 多个问题 **逐条都答**，按「从下往上」顺序
- 分条发，不要一大段长文
- 先做「靠谱顾问」再卖货，不要一上来逼单

**反面示范（真实，勿学）**
- 客户："Very dishonest" → 客服："Why would you think that?"（❌ 防御反问）
- 正确续接："I understand why you might feel this way… I'm sorry if my words gave you that impression. Whether to proceed is entirely your decision, I won't pressure you."（✅ 共情+降压）

## 一、效果类 ·「真的有用吗 / 多久见效 / 没感觉」

**客户高频原句**
- Does it really work? / I hope yours works?
- How long does it take to feel change?
- I'm not feeling anything, is the medicine working?

**应答公式**
```
认可顾虑 → 解释中药「渐进、先内后外」机制 → 给案例/视频证据 → 设合理预期(7-15天) → 留问号继续跟
```

**真实话术范例**
- 认可 + 预期："Two days is actually too early, so it's normal not to feel a noticeable change. Most men start to feel improvements after **7–15 days**, because the herbal ingredients need to accumulate gradually."
- 机制："Herbal conditioning improves **internally first** — blood circulation, energy, sleep — before gradually reflecting in sexual function."
- 对比西药："It's not like the Viagra type of quick fix, it's a gradual process of regaining function. I'll guide you through the whole process."
- 证据："You can look at their feedback videos (Google Drive link). If it didn't work, why would they risk their privacy to record a video?"

**禁止**
- 承诺「几天一定硬」等无法兑现的具体保证
- 忽略客户「没感觉」的焦虑直接催复购

## 二、信任类 ·「你是不是骗子 / FB 广告不可信 / 怎么证明」

**客户高频原句**
- I'm sceptical of anything sold on Facebook, 90% are fake.
- How can I trust what you are saying?
- I can't find your business online, do you have a website?

**应答公式**
```
共情(很多人都这样) → 区隔「我不是广告群发」→ 亮可验证证据 → 给售后/退款保障 → 留问号
```

**真实话术范例**
- 共情 + 区隔："I completely understand. Many people feel the same way. The difference is I'm **not selling a generic product** advertised everywhere — I tailor treatment to your condition, that's why I communicate directly instead of just sending a link."
- 资质（核心）："I am a **licensed Chinese medicine practitioner, registered with AHPRA, my registration number is CMR0002510454. You can verify my credentials.**"
- 法律背书："In Australia, all AHPRA-registered practitioners are subject to strict consumer protection laws — if treatment doesn't deliver, clients can file a complaint. That's why I only take cases I'm confident in."
- 公司信息："SYDNEY CHINESE MEDICINE CENTER, ABN 71 109 163 395" + 官网链接
- 透明（真实成交话术）："All my information is open and transparent, you can verify it any time."

**禁止**
- 防御性反问、与客户争论
- 疯狂证明公司却不共情

## 三、价格类 ·「多少钱 / 太贵 / 没预算」

**客户高频原句**
- How much in AUD?
- Too expensive / I don't have any budget at the moment.

**应答公式**
```
报 A/B 双方案(给台阶) → 价值锚定(稀缺/定制/永久 vs 西药持续花钱) → 比价(医院上万/副作用) → 降门槛(定金/分期/付一半发一半) → 不一味降价
```

**真实话术范例**
- 双方案：A 32天 原价800→**400 AUD**（13药100倍）；B 20天 原价600→**300 AUD**（8药50倍）
- 推荐："Based on your condition and symptoms, I recommend **Plan A**. What do you think?"
- 价值："A is comprehensive restoration with stronger effect on nervous system, testosterone and corpus cavernosum — suitable for severe or multiple issues."
- 折中（真实，应对「先付一点」）：客户 "$100 now, $300 if it works" → "I really can't ship for just $100. **If you could pay $200 upfront, I can mail you half the medication** so you feel results right away."
- 保障消解贵："No secondary charges. If after one course you're not satisfied, I can replace the program **or give a full refund** — this is my commitment and my confidence in the product."

**禁止**
- 客户一喊贵就立刻打折到底
- 没有价值铺垫只谈钱

## 四、拖延类 ·「我考虑一下 / 和妻子商量 / 过几天」

**客户高频原句**
- I'll think about it. / Let me find out more first.
- I have to talk to my wife before I go ahead.

**应答公式**
```
支持决定+共情 → (妻子)把她拉同阵营 → 得失对比 → 给具体时间锚点 → 低压力保持联系
```

**真实话术范例**
- 共情决定："That shows you're a thoughtful person who takes your health seriously, and that you love your wife. I respect your decision."
- 妻子同阵营："Some patients' partners may not initially see this as a medical issue, or feel it doesn't warrant the investment — which is understandable. That's why communication matters, to help her understand this affects your overall health and quality of life."
- 时间锚点 + 紧迫："I'll wait for your results **tomorrow at 11 a.m.** so I can **prepare your herbs in advance**."
- 破冰跟进："Good morning. Did you sleep well last night?" / "Are you at work right now?"

**禁止**
- 客户说考虑就消失（敬畏流量、不挂0）
- 持续高压逼单制造压迫

## 五、支付方式类 ·「怎么付 / 不想线上付卡 / 用 PayPal」

**客户高频原句**
- How do I pay you?
- I don't like giving card details online.
- Can you send PayPal?

**应答公式**
```
给两种(银行转账/信用卡链接) → 客户拒一种就立刻迁就另一种 → PayPal 强调退款保障 → 收款后要截图核对再发货
```

**真实话术范例**
- 给选择："We accept bank transfers and credit card payments. Which is more convenient for you?"
- 信用卡链接时效："I can generate a credit card payment link. Please pay now, the link **expires in 30 minutes**."
- 迁就（真实成交杠杆）：客户 "bank transfer instead" → "Okay, which bank do you usually use? I'll provide the account info."
- PayPal 信任："PayPal **holds your payment securely** and won't credit us until the parcel ships and tracking is confirmed — you're covered all the way. If I don't upload tracking by the next day, you can request an **unconditional refund**."
- 银行新账户提示安抚（真实）："Since you're transferring to a new account, the bank shows this prompt asking you to verify — this is normal, no need to worry."
- 收款核对："Please send me the **payment receipt** after you pay, and I'll start preparing your herbs."

**禁止**
- 死守一种支付方式逼客户
- 备注里写药物相关词汇（红线）

## 六、收货 / 物流类 ·「多久到 / 几盒 / 怎么吃」

**客户高频原句**
- How long to arrive? / How many tablets?
- How to use? Do I take with water?

**应答公式**
```
正面给数字 → 问城市估时效 → 收齐收货信息 → 24h内给单号+邮箱凭证 → 一对一指导服用
```

**真实话术范例**
- 数量 + 问城市："24 pills, 100x concentrated. **What city do you live in?** I can give an estimated delivery time."
- 收货模板：
  ```
  Name:
  Phone Number:
  Email:
  Shipping Address: (exact house number, for door-to-door)
  ZIP Code:
  ```
- 物流承诺："I'll send the searchable parcel info and tracking number to your email **within 24 hours**."
- 服用指导："Two capsules in the morning and two in the evening, **on an empty stomach before meals**." / "Just chew it, no need for water."
- 一对一钩子："Once you receive it, I'll give you **one-on-one instruction** on how to use it."

## 七、货到付款（COD）·「先发货我再付」

**客户高频原句**
- You'll have to send without payment and then I'll pay you.
- Can I pay on delivery?

**应答公式**
```
共情顾虑 → 解释定制药不可二次销售、无法先发 → 用 PayPal 退款保障替代 → 给定金台阶降风险
```

**真实话术范例**
- 共情 + 守底线："I completely understand — if I'd seen that bank message, I'd be cautious too. Regarding 'ship first, pay later', I hope you understand I **can't prepare and ship customized products without confirmation**."
- 替代方案："But **PayPal gives you extra protection** — pay via PayPal, I'll update tracking next day; if I fail, you get an **immediate unconditional refund**. It's fair and secure for both of us."
- 信任互惠："Trust is a two-way street. I already agreed to let you **pay half first, get half the medication** — normally we don't allow this. Just as I trust you, I hope you can trust me."

**禁止**
- 答应货到付款
- 只拒绝不给替代方案

## 八、退款 / 保障类 ·「没效果怎么办 / 能退吗」

**应答公式**
```
给明确承诺(不二次收费/免费换/全款退) → 关联资质(敢承诺=有信心) → 转化为信任而非纠结退款
```

**真实话术范例**
- "No secondary charges. If after one treatment cycle you're **not satisfied**, I can give a **free replacement program or a full refund**. This is my commitment and my confidence in our products."
- "I'm AHPRA-certified, number CMR0002510454. I only take cases I'm confident in."

## 九、服药后没效果 / 售后类（成交后，常被忽略但很关键）

> 真实样本里有客户成交后狂问效果、客服一度失联导致差点闹崩。**售后必须及时响应**。

**客户高频原句**
- I've used 2 days, nothing, no morning erection.
- The medicine is not working? Do I need another order?

**应答公式**
```
立刻回(勿失联) → 谢诚实+共情 → 重申渐进机制(先内后外) → 给微小进步信号当证据 → 引导坚持/续疗程 → 必要时复查归因(作息/吸烟/吸收)
```

**真实话术范例**
- 共情："I understand your concern. Thank you for being honest with me."
- 机制重申："Herbs don't react immediately like chemicals — it's step-by-step, repairing blood circulation and hormone balance first. Your body is adjusting internally."
- 头痛等反应安抚："The headache is a **temporary reaction when circulation is activated**, it usually goes away as the body balances."
- 续疗程引导："For results to stay consistent, I recommend another full course to **solidify progress** — if you stop halfway, the gains won't stabilize and your wife may remain dissatisfied."
- 归因（真实）："What else could cause poor absorption? 1. Late nights/drinking/smoking burden liver metabolism. 2. Anxiety/stress delays neurohormonal response. 3. Weak digestion weakens efficacy. Do you have any of these?"

**禁止（真实教训）**
- 已读不回、长时间失联（客户连发 "Why not replying??"）
- 把责任甩给客户「你没按说明吃」却又记错剂量 → 反而引发争执

## 十、症状机制类 ·「为什么我会这样 / 你怎么治」

> 用西医/药理逻辑讲，澳洲客户更听得懂；不堆中文术语。

**应答公式**
```
明确需求 → 定位问题 → 具象化(封闭式提问) → 科学解释 → 给方案(为什么能改善)
```

**真实话术范例**
- 具象化提问："How firm is your erection? Which stage in this diagram?（配硬度图）" / "How long has this been going on?"
- 机制（四层，真实）：
  - 内分泌："androgens are essential for libido and erection… 梅花鹿鞭、杜仲雄花、桑椹 optimize hormone/testosterone."
  - 血管："an erection lies in relaxation of corpus cavernosum smooth muscle… 野山参、蚕蛹粉 repair and dilate vessels."
  - 阴茎："promote vasodilation, reduce glans sensitivity… 葛根、肉桂、枸杞、肉苁蓉."
  - 前列腺："improve microcirculation, accelerate removal of inflammatory mediators."
- 确认理解："Does that make sense to you?"（每段后确认）

## 十一、逼单 / 跟单话术阶梯（报价后）

> 前提：过程走完、顾虑已解、分清重1(选了方案)/重2(没选)。

**阶梯公式**
```
福利赠品(限时) → 定金保留(50AUD起) → 情感垫资(有交换条件) → 每次带新理由,不空逼
```

**真实话术范例**
- 时间锚点 + 备药紧迫："I'll have your herbs ready tonight, waiting for your good news at 11 a.m."
- 付一半台阶："Pay $200 upfront, I'll mail half the medication so you feel results right away."
- 定金/垫资需 **先共情 + 说明稀缺/申请**，再提条件（如转介绍）
- 重2 跟单先让开口："是效果还是价格的顾虑？" 弄清再跟

**禁止**
- 报价前逼单、顾虑没解就逼单
- 反复同一句催、对观望客户过度施压
- 超时不降级（重1 七天 / 重2 三天后降标签）
